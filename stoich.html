Tropical Forest Foliar Stoichiometry

I collaborated with Molly Cavaleri, Paulo Olivas, Steve Oberbauer & my PhD advisor Stephen Porder to analyze foliar stoichiometry (10 elements!) across La Selva, Costa Rica. We used modular towers to comprehensively sample leaves, from forest floor to canopy top.
